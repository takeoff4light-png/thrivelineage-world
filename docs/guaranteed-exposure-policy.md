# Guaranteed Exposure (Policy / T&C) — Single Tier, Single Guaranteed Surface

## 1) Definitions (controllable + integrity-first)
**Guaranteed Exposure** means ThriveLineage will publish/place approved content on a **ThriveLineage-controlled surface** within the stated **Fulfillment Window**.

Guaranteed Exposure **does not** include outcomes or results, including (without limitation) sales, leads, impressions, clicks, conversions, rankings, engagement, virality, or PR pickup.

All publishing is subject to:
- **Basic content approval** (safety, legality, dignity-first standards), and
- **Human verification** (automation may draft; nothing is published without human review/approval).

## 2) The only guaranteed surface (what we control and can prove)
**Guaranteed surface:** ThriveLineage.world **Featured Placement** (the “Featured” surface on the ThriveLineage.world website).

Anything else—including listing/profile creation or updates, owned social, partners, newsletters, events, third-party media, third-party directories, or platform algorithms—is **optional** and **not** part of the guarantee unless explicitly stated as guaranteed in writing.

## 3) Proof of fulfillment (single standard)
For each guaranteed delivery item fulfilled, ThriveLineage issues a **Fulfillment Receipt** (email and/or in-account) containing:
- item type: **Site Featured Placement**
- live URL (or site path) to the featured placement
- publication timestamp (date/time)
- the billing month/cycle applied
- reviewer verification marker (initials or ID)

The Fulfillment Receipt is the proof of delivery.

## 4) Start event + fulfillment window
**Acceptance (start event):** the fulfillment clock begins when ThriveLineage confirms (in writing) that the submission is approved and ready for placement.

**Fulfillment Window:** ThriveLineage fulfills one Site Featured Placement within **30 days** of Acceptance for the applicable monthly cycle.

## 5) Default tier (single structure)
### ThriveLineage Featured (Monthly)
- **Included (guaranteed):** 1 Site Featured Placement per month on ThriveLineage.world
- **Proof:** Fulfillment Receipt

## 6) Scheduling + rollover cap (capacity-safe)
Placements are scheduled via a fair-rotation system based on readiness, approvals, and capacity.

If a monthly placement cannot be fulfilled within the Fulfillment Window due to capacity, it **rolls over for up to one (1) additional month** and is fulfilled at the earliest available slot. Rollovers do **not** accumulate beyond the one-month cap.

## 7) Edge constraints
- If the Featured surface is temporarily unavailable (site outage/maintenance), fulfillment is paused until it is publishable and linkable; timing is tracked against the Fulfillment Window from Acceptance.
- If required inputs are missing or revisions are requested by the client, Acceptance is not issued (or Acceptance is reissued after revisions), and the Fulfillment Window runs from the latest Acceptance.
- Content that fails basic approval (illegal, unsafe, dignity-violating) is not eligible for Acceptance or guaranteed placement.
