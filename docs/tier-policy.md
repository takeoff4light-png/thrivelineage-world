# Tier Policy & Ethical Guarantees (Draft)

## Definitions
- "Exposure" means: POSTS PUBLISHED (not impressions, not sales).
- "Guarantee" means: X approved posts will be published within Y days.
- Exclusions: platform outages, account restrictions/bans, policy rejections, missing client approvals.

## Freedom of choice
Clients can:
- approve/reject drafts before publishing
- pause scheduling
- choose platforms
- revoke permissions anytime

## We never promise
- sales, virality, follower growth
- outcomes outside platform/user control

## We do promise
- an auditable activity log
- transparent delivery tracking against the defined guarantee
